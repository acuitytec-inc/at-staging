AcuityTec - A Fintech Power 50 Member

[Montreal, Quebec, December 6, 2023] - AcuityTec, a KYC and fraud prevention provider, announces it is a Fintech Power 50 member by Fintech Futures - recognizing the fintech industry's most influential, innovative, and powerful figures. AcuityTec's inclusion in this list underscores their unwavering dedication to revolutionizing how businesses access and process identity verification data, transactional monitoring and adaptive fraud defence. Their solutions ensure businesses easily leverage a complete end-to-end fraud mitigation platform to be agile, scalable, and predictive against new online threats and sophisticated fraudsters.

“This year’s Fintech Power 50 cohort of companies are as diverse as ever in terms of which sectors they’re disrupting and where in the world they are disrupting them,” explains Jason Williams, co-founder of The Fintech Power 50. “They are having an impact in regtech, paytech, wealthtech, banking-as-a-service, lendtech and open banking, providing some of the most exciting models of disruption.”

"In an era where online transactions are not just the norm but rapidly accelerating, our vision at AcuityTec has always been to challenge the status quo in the fintech industry and provide cutting-edge, reliable and efficient fraud defence solutions. Being recognized as a part of the Fintech Power 50 is not just an honor; it's a validation of our commitment to innovation and excellence," states Alfredo Solis, Senior Director and Business Strategist at AcuityTec.

Mark Walker, co-founder of The Fintech Power 50, also commented: “While our lively members are busy solving pain points for customers and the industry right now, there are also 10 finfluencers in this year’s cohort who are looking forward to future trends within the financial services sector. Being part of the Fintech Power 50 is not just an award, it’s a testament to the work individuals and companies are doing to help the sector innovate and grow, and also serves as a great recommendation tool.”

The recognition by Fintech Power 50 highlights AcuityTec's contribution to creating a global eco-system for safe, secure, and reliable financial transactions. The company's solutions transcend geographical limitations, presenting itself as a perfect collaborator for businesses growing on an international scale or those operating globally. AcuityTec's KYC data hub and advanced technology enable businesses to achieve thorough identity verification, confidently manage the intricacies of global regulatory demands, reduce risks, and ensure seamless transactional processes.

AcuityTec's solutions dynamically adapt to the evolving landscape of online fraud. They understand that as technology advances, so do the techniques used by fraudsters. "Our solutions are built to be responsive, predictive and constantly evolving to stay ahead of the curve. From detecting fraud patterns and trends to account associations, advanced risk screening, and transactional monitoring layered with machine learning, perpetual KYC and data science, our platform gives businesses a holistic 360-degree view of their risk. Empowering them to make quantifiable decisions and propel growth." adds Solis.

In conclusion, AcuityTec's inclusion in the Fintech Power 50 is a significant milestone, reflecting the company's dedication to excellence in the fintech industry. "Being part of the Fintech Power 50 propels us into a new realm of possibilities and opens doors to new partnerships to expand our global footprint in making our online eco-system stronger and more trustworthy," explains Solis. "This recognition is just the beginning. We are committed to continuing our journey of innovation and excellence, driving forward the future of fintech."
