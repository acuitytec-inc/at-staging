Introducing AcuityTec PayoutPro: Elevating Operational Integrity with Innovative Payout Solutions.

[Montreal, Quebec, April 24th, 2024] (Newswire.com) - AcuityTec, a leader in modern fraud prevention and KYC platform, is excited to announce the launch of its AcuityTec PayoutPro, a specialized solution designed to optimize payout verification and risk analysis efficiency for the iGaming industry. Built on AcuityTec's proven transactional monitoring framework, PayoutPro integrates flawlessly to secure players and their associated payout transactions, enhancing efficiency and boosting player trust.

"Our AcuityTec PayoutPro is a game-changer in iGaming technology," said Alfredo Solis, Managing Director of AcuityTec. By enhancing customer verification and implementing dedicated parameters specific to payouts, PayoutPro sets new industry standards for fraud defense and operational risk assessments for account reconciliations."

PayoutPro introduces sophisticated features designed to enhance security, speed, and confidence in payout transactions by implementing unique verifications tailored specifically for payouts, effectively combating common fraud tactics such as account takeovers. Moreover, PayoutPro streamlines operations within payouts and accounts reconciliation teams by automating customer verification and payout transactional risk screening. This automation ensures that payouts are swiftly directed to verified customers based on the most current KYC data-driven results, eliminating the need for prolonged human intervention. Ultimately, these enhancements bolster customer trust by guaranteeing that payouts are securely processed and delivered to authenticated recipients, fostering satisfaction and long-term player loyalty.

"Trust and efficiency are critical in today's digital landscape," Solis emphasized. "PayoutPro is uniquely equipped to meet these challenges and includes 15 KYC verification options, including geo check, address verification, phone, email, database identity match verifications, and more. All in all, operators can conduct thorough identity checks for every payout transaction in a customized on-demand manner depending on their risk needs."

Operators receive real-time updates on payout processing health, ensuring swift and informed decision-making."

In addition to the launch of PayoutPro, AcuityTec has enhanced its platform with several notable upgrades:

PIV Enhancements: Improved control with stage-specific upload settings, mobile verification enforcement, and enriched reporting capabilities.

Email Notifications: Enhanced alerts with detailed transaction and registration data, facilitating instant rule-triggered updates without requiring logins.

Platform Experience: User-centric improvements include refined report behaviors, expanded date filters, customizable export options, and upgraded visual elements for enhanced usability.
