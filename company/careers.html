Why Us

Be a Part of Change

Founded in 2011, we're a global leader in risk management and changing the game to bring simplicity, flexibility, automation and data-driven orchestration to combat global fraud. Protecting our customers, businesses and their consumers in our modern online world with trust, clarity and confidence.

We're always looking for people with sharp minds and a passion for their talent to be a part of our mission of improving online security and engagement, trust, and value, creating a positive impact in our world.

Passion

Are you passionate about data, protecting identities, making a difference and driving trust in our online world? Are you passionate about what you do and what you offer? If yes, then we would love to hear from you.

Growth

Leaders are not just born. They are created. We are dedicated to working with you and supporting your skills. As part of our team, you'll gain valuable experience, exposure and freedom to grow your talent.

Collaboration

We value that more brains are better than one. Our culture is driven by open and transparent collaboration, working together for one goal in supporting our products, customers and overall mission for a better online world.

Flexibility

Our talent is located worldwide with the freedom and flexibility to work remotely. It is more than just putting in the hours; our people focus on the quality of productive output and the ability to hold themselves accountable.
